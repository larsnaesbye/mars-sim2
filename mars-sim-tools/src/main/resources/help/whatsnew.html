  6. Goods Manager: rework relationship with objectives.

  9. Heating: revamp heat sink & ventilation.
  10. Medical: rework prescribing medication, examining bodies, and recovery tasks.
  11. Preferences: manage settlement preferences in various categories of mission, science and task.

  5. Health Log: show a person's health history log with complaint types, date started and date cured.

  10. Vehicle: shows highlighted vehicle box border in settlement map.

  1. Airlock: correctly looks for airlock with least number of occupants.
  2. Heating: manage temperature instability via entropy change to relate temperature change and heat transfer.
  3. Launch Date: correct the launch day calculation when it's before 01-Adir-01.
  4. Navigation: rework vehicle movement to consume fuel and battery power correctly.
  5. Parking: set limits in looking for vehicle parking locations recursively.
  6. Power Generation: correct how power grid turns on/off buildings during power surplus/deficit.
  7. Resource: correct budgeting essential resource on-demand.
  8. Resource: resolve dwindling amount of methane and hydrogen.
  9. Robot Charging: avoid robot battery depletion. Ensure wireless charging always possible.
  10. Salvage Process: Correct problem when scoring the salvage of a vehicle.
